Speed–Stability Tradeoff

Stability degrades sharply above 260 km/h. At 360+ km/h the average stability index drops to just 53 — nearly half of samples show instability at top speed.

🔧

DRS: Drag Slayer

DRS cuts average drag by 25% (179→135 N) while barely affecting downforce. This confirms DRS acts as a pure drag-reduction mechanism without compromising grip.

📐

Wing Angle Sweet Spot

Downforce scales linearly with wing angle, but so does drag. The aero efficiency ratio is relatively flat, suggesting wing angle is mostly a downforce-vs-drag tradeoff dial.
